Jackbot Games

Discord bot for gaming in discord. Has Zany™ games such as Minesweeper, Rock Paper Scissors, and Tic Tac Toe! Check it out!

Specifics?

Minesweeper - j!minesweeper {width} {length} {mines}. Defaults to 6 6 7.

Rock Paper Scissors - j!rps @{other person}. Sends you a DM with which you react with 🗿, 📰, or ✂️.

Tic Tac Toe - j!tictactoe @{other person}. Sends a playable tic tac toe grid.

Connect 4 - j!connectfour @{other person}. Sends a playable connect four grid. Send a number 1-7 and the tile drops down.
